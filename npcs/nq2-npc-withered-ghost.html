NeoQuest II: Non-Player Characters

Non-Player characters are those characters that you can't play as - they mainly talk or trade with you, or give quests.

Meridell | Terror Mountain | Lost Desert | Haunted Woods | Faerieland

Withered ghost
Lost City of Phorofor (View Map) (Act 2)
It means you no harm, but you might want to get the Key first.
Abilities: talk, quest
Conversation
The withered ghost looks at you balefully. "This is a place of danger, and of death. None who enter shall leave. And none shall enter who do not know the key."
You say, "What key?"
The withered ghost shakes his head. "The key is long lost. Only those who are trustworthy may learn it. Seek those who are wise, in the Eye of Meridell beyond the north of the desert."
You say, "I know the key -- 'Ad ro un ta en.'"
The withered ghost smiles grimly. "So you wish to enter. So be it." The ghost fades away, and the huge sandstone blocks before you fade away into nothingness. The path into the city lies open.
