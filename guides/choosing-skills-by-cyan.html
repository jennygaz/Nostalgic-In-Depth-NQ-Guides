Choosing Skills

NeoQuest guide by Cyan Cloud.

Well, after completing Neoquest a number of times and surveying people from the Neoquest chat boards, I can't help but notice that people always say that the magic they specialise in are always the best, or so as they claim. I've got a friend who thinks Fire Magic is the best skill you can get, excluding Shock, that is. Personally, I think Fire Magic is not really effective as what he thinks. Think about it, after you look at my Magic and Skill review.

  • Fire Magic: Not very strong compared to the few others. Attacks only does a small amount of damage or needs to be casted, which I think is a waste of a turn as you have to do an attack that could be resisted whereas a normal attack would have a higher chance of hitting and does more damage.
    • Fire Weapons: Only does a few points of damage, not very effective.
    • Firepower: About the same as Fire Weapons.
    • Fireball: A casting attack, wasting a turn again.
    • Wall of Flame: Not a bad skill to have, considering it attacks your opponent when you're hit and you missed your opponent. But, wasting all those points on those other skills? You decide. I might not do it if I were in your shoes.
  • Ice Magic: About the same as Fire Magic but a little bit worse as most of the skills there are more easily resisted than some Fire attacks.
    • Ice Weapons: Same as Fire Weapons.
    • Heart of Ice: Only has a low percentage of 'freezing' your opponent.
    • Snowball: Another casting attack.
    • Glacier Strike: Does tons of damage and would be great if it wasn't resisted most of the time.
  • Shock Magic: The best Magic you can have, or so as I claim(see, told you!). You don't have to waste a turn by casting anything and Shock Magic can be offensive and defensive as well.
    • Shock Weapons: Stuns your opponents for a round or so, not a waste of skills as it's only the first skill and it's that much better than the first skills of Fire and Ice.
    • Disable: Only does a few extra points of damage but the next skills are awesome(you need it to get to those other skills!).
    • Fortitude: Boosts your resistance against Elemental blasters, stuns, poisoned blasts, etc. A smashing skill to get.
    • Shockwave: My favorite skill(oops, I'm claiming again!). Stuns and does damage to your opponents, smashing, just smashing.
  • Spectral Magic: Not a bad Magic, boosts up your defense and might reflect damage too!
    • Spectral Weapons: Only works with a Spectral Weapon at hand. You need it for those other skills again.
    • Evasion: Evades attacks. Not a bad skill.
    • Absorption: Hope I've spelt it correctly! Another casting skill, but the skill which is awesome in my eyes is still one more point away(claiming again. Now you know what I mean?).
    • Reflex: Smashing! It really does let your opponent's attack smash into themselves! I like seeing Jahbal hitting himself for 57 damage!
  • Life Magic: The basic unit. A definite must-have. Heals you while you're attacking and moving(you wouldn't want to end up without any potions left and losing to Xantan The Foul, right?). Why am I talking about him(or it)? We'll touch on that later.
    • Life Weapons: Increases the value of potions. Not bad.
    • Field Medic: Heals you when you're walkin' around.
    • Lifesteal: Heals you but dosen't steal life from your opponents. Weird day, isn't it?
    • Resurrection: Never gonna need if you're not gonna be defeated!

Tip time: Don't waste skill points on skills that rely on combat success by your opponent to work and as I mentioned earlier, try to avoid the skills which need to be cast, or wasting a turn, if you ask me. Take a positive attitude during combat, if played properly, those skills are never needed.
